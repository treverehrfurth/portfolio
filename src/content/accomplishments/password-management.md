Critical credentials for Finance, HR, Sales, and external systems were being shared through Excel files and informal channels — no governance, no auditability, and persistent access risk after employees departed. Deployed Bitwarden as the enterprise password management platform:

- Rolled out to Advanced Analytics as the primary delivery team, with staged expansion as other departments come up for air
- Secured with Entra ID SSO so access follows the employee lifecycle
- Established administrative recovery controls to preserve access to non-SSO external systems
- Enabled stronger password hygiene and integrated MFA that reduces reliance on personal devices

Result: secure vault-based credential storage, cleaner offboarding for non-SSO apps, and a scalable foundation for broader rollout across Finance, HR, and the rest of the org.
