The tenant had no guardrails on Group or site creation, leaving hundreds of unused, duplicate, and abandoned Teams and SharePoint sites with no clear ownership. Led a cleanup and governance initiative:

- Audited and consolidated active departmental workspaces, retiring abandoned and duplicate sites
- Restricted Microsoft 365 Group creation via PowerShell to an approved admin security group
- Established baseline governance standards for group and site lifecycle
- Laid the groundwork for sustainable collaboration management going forward

Result: reduced collaboration confusion, tightened permission hygiene, and safeguards in place to prevent future uncontrolled growth.
